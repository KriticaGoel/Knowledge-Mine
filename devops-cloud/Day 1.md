What is DevOps?
>DevOps is a culture and set of practices that brings development and operations together to automate software delivery. Its goal is to deliver reliable software faster by using practices such as continuous integration, continuous delivery, infrastructure as code, containerization, and monitoring.

What are Benefits of DevOps?
1. Automated
2. Repeatable 
3. Faster 
4. Less error-prone
5. Deployments can happen in minutes. 
6. Rollbacks are easier. 
7. Testing is automated. 
8. Teams release features more frequently.
